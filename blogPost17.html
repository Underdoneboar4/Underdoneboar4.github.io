Iteration and testing (life cycle).

#WordJournal

Main Content

Iteration Testing is all about taking one version of a thing, finding it's issues, and building to try and minimize those issues (or get rid of them entirely). “Failing early and often” applies especially to testing, with a good goal of having many iterations, because it is easier to test individual changes instead of batches of changes all at once. Good testing leads to better iterating! This is important for me to remember in my career because iterating on ideas and making sure smaller things get the testing they deserve is an important part of the software development process. https://www.interaction-design.org/literature/article/don-t-make-me-think-key-learning-points-for-ux-design-for-the-web
