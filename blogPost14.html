Standardization. Prototyping in Figma is all about finding the right style to use for your product and expanding that style across an entire (semi)functional prototype. Figma is designed to make components and styles easier to expand and edit later on. You are also able to implement standard design features like pie charts or geometric shapes. Instead of having to repeatedly copy-paste assets and elements, you are able to have items that are treated more like child objects whose properties change in accordance with their parents. This is important to remember in my future career because design work can be much, much harder to standardize without being able to implement the features that Figma provides. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Saz6S1svYns
