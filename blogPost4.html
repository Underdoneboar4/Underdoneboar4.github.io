In this reading, I read about and learned about techniques for optimizing slide-based presentations. Some points mentioned include the importance of image-based and visual communication, the signal-to-noise ratio, the drawbacks of bullet points and bullet point conventions, and the importance of empty space. For each concept, we read about improvements to slides that lack a certain feature.
