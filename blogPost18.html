Refutation. In this reading, Steve Krug addresses common mistaken comments and ideas that supervisors and executives have. He provides example emails addressing two common sentiments detailing his thoughts. He pretty throughly refutes the arguments presented, like that a website might want to collect more info than necessary. He details a scenario of a user finding out that something simple has turned into a “project” for them, and that causing them to leave the website. This point specifically is important to remember in my career because getting more info than necessary can be very tempting.
