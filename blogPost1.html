Main Content

Signifiers are to affordances as instruction manuals are to IKEA furniture. The instructions show you how to use the various items in the box to make whatever furniture you wanted. They should ideally be easy to understand and relatively intuitive. Signifiers are the same way, but are much more subtle, and let users see how to use the given affordances to do something they want. This is important for me to remember in my career because recognizing differences between signifiers and affordances can help me better classify them in a job environment involving design. Norman, D. (2013). The design of everyday things. Basic Books.
