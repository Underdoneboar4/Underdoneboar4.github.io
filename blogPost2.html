Section: The Visceral Level Word: evolutionary The “lizard brain” part of us came from the most old fashioned survival instincts. The lowest level of processing, it makes up our subconscious fight or flight response. I chose the word “evolutionary” because these responses are deeply embedded into our brains by evolutionary selection. We can’t (really) control them, they are as much a part of our physical selves as us standing on two legs. This concept is important to remember in my career because it represents a base part of myself that I have to acknowledge and work with (and around) to be able to consciously make the right decisions. Norman, D. (2013). The design of everyday things. Basic Books.
